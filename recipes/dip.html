T. Arnolds World Famous Onion Dip

T. Arnold's World Famous French Onion Dip

Background

Don't even bother sitting down cause you're just gonna stand up once you get a taste of this onion dip. This is, hands down, the best combination of easy and delicious dip in the world. 4 ingredients total, and literally all you do is throw them in a bowl, toss that in the oven, and bam.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion (smaller side)
  • 1 cup shaved/grated parmesean cheese
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 block of cream cheese

Steps

  1. Get everything on your counter. We want that cream cheese to come up to room temp
  2. Set your oven to pre-heat to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Chop up that there onion real good
  4. Throw all of the ingredients into a big bowl, and get them well mixed
  5. Throw all that into a 9x9 glass pan
  6. Throw that pan into your preheated oven for, eh, 40-45 minutes or until some nice leoparding on top
  7. Take it out to cool for a bit, and enjoy
