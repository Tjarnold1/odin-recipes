T. Arnolds World Famous Chili

Description

A staple in the T. Arnold's World Famous recipe book. This is, potentially, the biggest guy chili recipe there ever was, or ever will be. Like most T. Arnold cooking, it's exteremly low effort, and low skill. Also like most, it could probably be better. Oh well.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs 93% lean ground turkey
  • 1 pack McCormick's Regular Chili Seasoning
  • 1 T. Arnold's World Famous measuring bowl of tomatoes
  • 2 six oz cans of Hunt's Tomato paste
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 can salted whole kernel corn
  • 1 cup of dried black beans
  • Adobo seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil your black beans to absolute PERFECTION while also eyeballing the perfect amount of Adobo seasoning
  2. Chop up them tomatoes and put them in your measuring bowl
  3. Chop up them bell peppers too while you're at it. Put them in a separate bowl with your beans
  4. Open up them cans of corn and tomato paste. We're usin it all
  5. Toss the turkey into a fat pot, and stir the McCormicks seasoning into it
  6. Set it over medium heat, and get the turkey like 80% cooked
  7. Toss in the tomatoes, let em soften up for a few minutes
  8. Throw in all of the tomato paste. Let that carmelize for a little bit, maybe a minute or two
  9. Throw in the bell peppers, beans, and corn. Lower the heat to medium-low, cover, and let cook for 4.5 minutes
  10. Stir it up, and let it go for another 4.5 minutes.
  11. Enjoy you WINNER.
